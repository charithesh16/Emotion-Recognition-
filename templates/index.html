About Us

Ever felt like nobody understands you or what you are going through. Well we have got you. In this day and age, AI has edged into our lives more than we could possibly comprehend. Our main objective is to blend two of the most indigenous yet simple human outcomes, AI and our emotions! This application primarily focuses on analyzing the facial expressions of the user. And all you have got to do is well, express yourself!

Know More about our idea

  • 01What is Emotion Analysis

    Emotion Analysis aims to detect and recognize types of feelings through the expression of texts, such as anger, disgust, fear, happiness, sadness. Facial emotion recognition is the process of detecting human emotions from facial expressions.

  • 02Why do you need our application?

    Well simple, this eliminates the need to manually go to a therapist or a fellow human to pour out your emotions. More specifically it caters well to people who are introverted and/or not especially comfortable at expressing themselves.

    A human undergoes several emotions throughout the day, and few harsh instances can spoil the person’s mood. It could lead to *trigger alert* depression/ anger/ fear.

    And sometimes you desperately looking for a change of scene, maybe listening to a song or two could uplift you or a simple breathing regime could do the trick. That is where this application comes in handy, without having to search through the internet, you can find it all in one place.

  • 03Real Time Applications

    To improve the user’s experience, as a customer, learner, or as a generic service user and help improve services without asking the user formally and continuously for feedback.

    Emotion Recognition also finds applications in crowd analytics, social media, marketing, public safety, human- computer interaction, digital security surveillance, street analytics, etc.

    The point being, using automatic emotion recognition in public safety, healthcare, or assistive technology, can significantly improve the quality of people’s lives, allowing them to live in a safer environment and reducing the impact of a possible breakdown.
